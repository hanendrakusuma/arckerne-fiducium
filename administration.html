Services & Scope

Mandate‑based, non‑advisory administration only. No investment, tax, or legal advice is provided.

CAT‑01 · Private Trust Administration

Minute books, resolutions, and audit‑grade records

Maintenance of minute books, registers, and mandate files; ratification of instructions; preparation and coordination of annual financial statements with external counsel and auditors.

CAT‑02 · Corporate Mandate Governance

Board support for holding and treasury vehicles

Convening and documenting board decisions, maintaining statutory registers, and supporting governance protocols for Singapore and multi‑jurisdictional corporate structures.

CAT‑03 · Substance & Infrastructure

Economic substance for family and holding platforms

Provision of registered office, independent directors, board procedures, and local record‑keeping to support Singapore economic substance and controlled‑foreign‑company analysis for family offices and holding entities.

CAT‑08 · Documentation & Language Governance

Institutional calibration of written materials

Institutional calibration of written materials used within fiduciary and governance contexts, including mandate descriptions, internal policy documentation, and client-facing disclosures. This service focuses on linguistic clarity, regulatory neutrality, and consistency with non-advisory fiduciary administration standards. No marketing strategy, solicitation content, or public promotional activity is provided.

* Provided as editorial and structural review only. No legal opinion is given.

Scope of engagement

Services are provided on an administrative and governance basis only, within documented mandates agreed with clients’ legal and tax advisors. No investment management, product distribution, or retail onboarding is offered.

Legal & scope

ArcKernē Fiducium is a private fiduciary administration platform operating through a Singapore registered office. It does not accept public deposits and does not provide investment, tax, or legal advice.

ArcKernē Fiducium operates solely as an administrative and execution interface. Governance architecture and strategic design are provided by ArcKernē Structura under separate mandate.
